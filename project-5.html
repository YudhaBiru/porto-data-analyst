TokoBli Campaign Evaluation & Product Page Analysis

Project Summary

TokoBli, Indonesia's largest e-commerce company, experiences massive transaction surges during "twin date" sales campaigns. As a Data Analyst, I evaluated three past campaign periods (10/10, 11/11, 12/12) featuring different strategies to identify the most effective approach for future events. The analysis focuses on balancing discount costs with total revenue generated.

Campaign 12/12 achieved the highest efficiency with a 58.54:1 revenue-to-discount ratio, while Group B’s new page design increased average transaction value by 11.31%. I recommend 100% implementation of the new design, adopting a hybrid budgeting approach to maximize efficiency and sales volume, and segmenting discount strategies by category to capitalize on top performers like Men's Fashion.

Scope Of Work / Achievement

- Analyzed three major sales campaigns to identify that Campaign 12/12 yielded a peak 58.54:1 revenue-to-discount efficiency ratio.

- Cleaned and processed a dataset of 10,142 rows by removing outliers and duplicates to ensure accurate statistical reporting.

- Validated a new product detail page design using A/B testing, confirming an 11.31% increase in average transaction value.

- Identified Men's Fashion as the top category, achieving the highest transaction volume and revenue across all analyzed campaign periods.

Recommendations

- 100% Implementation of new product page design, since the A/B testing results yielded a p-value less than 0.05, the null hypothesis was rejected, proving the new design is statistically superior.

- Adopt a hybrid campaign budgeting strategy. This involves combining the high-efficiency tactics of the 12/12 campaign with the high-scale reach of the largest campaigns to balance cost-effectiveness with market volume.

- The analysis identified that categories do not perform equally. You should segment discount levels by category performance.

- Prioritize High-Efficiency Discount Ratios, Future campaign planning should model its discount structures after the 12/12 period to ensure that every IDR spent on discounts generates maximum return in total revenue.

Tools and Method

Tools : Excel, XLMiner

Method : Data Cleaning, Descriptive Analysis, A/B Testing, Pivot Table, EDA.
