RevoGrocers Sales Performance Analysis

Project Summary

The primary objective is to identify the highest-performing product categories. This is achieved by analyzing key metrics, including total revenue after discounts, total units sold, the number of unique customers, repeat purchase rates (RPR), and average price per unit across the product catalog.

Confections is the top-performing category, generating 12.87% of overall revenue and achieving the highest repeat purchase rate of 99.85%. Data shows lower prices attract more unique customers; therefore, RevoGrocers should expand the business, focus marketing on promising categories like Confections and Meat, and consider strategic price adjustments.

Scope Of Work / Achievement

- Analyzed product performance using SQL to identify Confections as the top category with a 99.85% repeat purchase rate.

- Evaluated revenue data to determine that the Confections category contributes 12.87% to the total revenue after discounts.

- Identified the highest-value customer (ID 94800) who completed 103 transactions totaling 134,409.83 in transaction value.

- Correlated average price per unit with customer volume, finding that lower-priced categories like Shellfish attract the most unique customers.

Recommendations

- Expand market : Because of high RPR, RevoGrocers should really expand the business.

- Category focused marketing : RevoGrocers should focus marketing on the promising category like confections and meat.

- Price adjusting : Data shows that the lower the price the more unique customers.

Tools and Method

Tools : SQL, GoogleBigQuery

Method : Data Transformation, Descriptive Statistics (Total Revenue, Average Price), Correlation Analysis (Price vs. Unique Customers), Repeat Purchase Rate (RPR) Calculation, Customer Segmentation (Top User Identification)
