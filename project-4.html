RevoFinance : Expense & Budget Utilization Dashboard

Project Summary

RevoFinance provides a dashboard solution for personal finance management to help app users understand spending patterns, monitor expenses, and conduct strategic planning. By analyzing personal expense data, the dashboard offers a clear view of where capital is allocated and how these expenditures align with the established annual budget across various merchant categories.

The average transaction value is Rp508,510, with December showing a 36.1% spending surge due to holiday effects, making Q4 the most volatile quarter. While four categories exceed their annual budget, the overall budget remains unoptimized; users should implement tighter controls during high-cost months and adjust category allocations to better match actual spending.

Scope Of Work / Achievement

- Developed a financial dashboard analyzing personal expenses to track and visualize an average transaction value of Rp508,510.

- Identified that 4 product categories exceeded annual budget limits, while 1 category reached exactly 100% utilization.

- Measured a 36.1% surge in spending during December, pinpointing Q4 as the most volatile and high-cost quarter.

- Mapped merchant locations and filtered top 5 merchants to analyze the percentage of individual merchant spending versus total amount.

Recommendations

- Move the leftover money from the "quiet" categories to cover the ones where user spent too much. This keeps the total yearly plan on track without needing to find extra cash.

- Set a notification on user phone for any purchase that is much higher than user usual average (which is about Rp508.510)

- Label spending categories as either "Essential" (like bills and groceries) or "Extra" (like eating out or hobbies). By organizing shops into groups, it becomes easier to see what is a necessity and what is just for fun.

- Prepare for the "December Spending Rush". The data shows that spending usually hits its highest point in December (Rp54.4M), which is much higher than other months. This is often due to holiday travel, gifts, or year-end celebrations.

Tools and Method

Tools : Python, GeminiAI (segment naming assistance)

Method : Data Cleaning & Transformation, RFM Segmentation (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) , Feature Engineering (adding Age, Retired Flag, and DTI Ratio columns), Correlation Analysis (Credit Limit vs. Age)
