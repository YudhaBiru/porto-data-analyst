RevoBank Customer Segmentation

Project Summary

RevoBank is an Indonesian bank aiming to increase credit card usage among existing customers. This project analyzes a six-month data summary from the Management Information System (MIS) to summarize sales performance and create detailed user personas for the Performance Management Team.

Analysis revealed a net profit of Rp 5.8 billion and a low fraud rate of 0.22% over six months. The "Potential Loyalist" segment is the largest group, comprising 54% of unique cards, while "Champions" represent less than 5%. Recommendations include targeting active users who show higher revenue potential compared to retired segments.

Scope Of Work / Achievement

- Cleaned and standardized datasets by correcting datatypes for 10 card columns and 5 user columns to ensure analytical accuracy.

- Processed 5,599 transaction records, removing 71 invalid or duplicate rows to maintain a high-quality data foundation.

- Calculated a total net profit of Rp 5,839,774,674 for a six-month period based on a 1.5% MDR rate.

- Segmented the customer base using RFM analysis, identifying that 54% of customers are Potential Loyalists for targeted marketing..

Recommendations

- Focus to turn Potential loyalist into Loyal user by giving them bonus, mail recommendation, discount, and promo.

- Please the Loyal user by giving them achievable points to use on airports, lounge, cashbacks, awards, and loyalty program so they could turn to Champions user.

- Decrease credit limit on inactive user like Hibernating and About to sleep user.

- Focus more on active user than the retired ones.

Tools and Method

Tools : Python, GeminiAI (segment naming assistance)

Method : Data Cleaning & Transformation, RFM Segmentation (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) , Feature Engineering (adding Age, Retired Flag, and DTI Ratio columns), Correlation Analysis (Credit Limit vs. Age)
